How to Delete Your Chezz Account

At Chezz, we respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to hey.singhdevs@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request."
  2. Include your account email address in the body of the email.
  3. We will process your request and delete your account within 7 days.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Deleted data: Your email address, password, Google ID (if used), game history, chess ratings, and any other personal information associated with your account.
  • Retained data: We do not retain any data related to your account once it is deleted.
  • Retention period: All personal data will be deleted within 7 days of your request.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need assistance with the account deletion process, please contact us at hey.singhdevs@gmail.com.

For more information on how we handle your data, please see our Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.
